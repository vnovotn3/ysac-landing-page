    What is YSAC App?

    The YSAC App is designed to simplify the organization of YSA conferences.

    For Participants:

    We offer a mobile app for both Android and iOS, providing key conference details such as schedules, contact lists, chats, files, and more. These features are aimed at enhancing the overall conference experience. See the full list of features.

    For Organizers:

    We provide a Web Admin platform that helps manage registrations, send emails and notifications, and control the content displayed on the mobile app. The Web Admin streamlines the registration process with customizable registration forms and a participant list that can easily be embedded into your conference website. See the full list of features.

    Mobile App Features

    • Conference Info

      Your one-stop hub for all essential participant details. Admins can easily add custom links, create informative pages, and showcase key contacts for conference organizers.

    • Chats & Notifications

      The app includes an announcements chat, where only admins can post updates, along with multiple group chats based on conference activities. Users receive notifications for new messages and upcoming scheduled events.

    • Schedule & Orientation

      A key feature of the app is the schedule, which displays daily activities with details on time, locations, and additional descriptions. A venue map can also be included alongside the schedule.

    • Groups & Contacts

      During the conference, participants can join or be assigned to various groups. A chat is automatically created for each group, allowing members to see and interact with others in their group. Each participant has a profile with the option to display their contact information.

    • Profile & Files

      Users can customize their profile by selecting which contact information to share and optionally adding social media links. Admins can also upload and share various conference materials with participants through the Files tab.
